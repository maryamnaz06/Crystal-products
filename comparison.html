Crystal Products Comparison

Check out the comparison of jewellery products featuring amber, diamonds, and amethyst.

Diamond Crystal

  • Diamonds are precious gemstones known for their brilliance and hardness.
  • It is typically colorless (can be yellow, blue, etc.)
  • Its often symbolizes love and commitment, commonly used in engagement rings and high-end jewelry.
  • Diamonds demands are high, often marketed as luxury items, stable investment.
  • Their price range vary from $1,000 to $10,000+.

Amber Crystal

  • Amber is fossilized tree resin known for its warm colors and natural inclusions.
  • It is Warm, organic look; often has inclusions and can be found in various colors (yellow, brown).
  • Associated with healing and protection, used in both decorative and holistic jewelry.
  • Amber is the most budget-friendly, making it accessible to a wide range of consumers.
  • Their price range vary from $20 to $200 (with rare pieces are higher).

Amethyst Crystal

  • Amethyst are beautiful purple gemstones, often associated with tranquility.
  • It is vibrant purple, ranging from light lavender to deep violet; can be cut into various shapes.
  • Amethyst Known for its calming properties, often used in spiritual or healing jewelry.
  • Amethyst offers a beautiful, colorful option that's versatile and moderately priced.
  • Their price range vary from $100 to $900 (higher for premium quality).
